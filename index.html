The Icon

From classic RnB, to the hottest current music from around the world, Problematic Black Hottie (aka Nyambura Njee) is one of Detroit’s leading premier selectors and tastemakers. For PBH, her outstanding selecting skills plus acute ability too curate a contagious vibe, combined with a dubbed “Iconic” aesthetic are her defining factor. With a consistently signature style, sound, look, and energy, Problematic Black Hottie has become a Detroit staple and household name in less than two years.
